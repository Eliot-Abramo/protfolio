Principles I Work By

  • Design the failure paths first. Timeouts, CRCs, asserts — demos shouldn’t be adrenaline sports.
  • Constraints are fuel. Power, latency, EMI — edges that force elegant solutions.
  • If others can’t maintain it, it isn’t done. Docs, diagrams and clear APIs matter.
  • Measure > guess. VNAs, logic analyzers, profilers — truth lives in traces.
