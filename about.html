About Me

I’m an **Embedded Systems & Avionics Engineer** driven by the intersection of elegant physics and rugged real‑world hardware.

* **Analog Neural Networks @ EPFL** – designed a phase‑tunable metasurface and an optical GELU emulator to slash the energy budget of deep learning. * **Lead Systems Engineer @ EPFL Xplore Rover** – architected a CRC‑hardened SPI network linking STM32 & ESP32 avionic nodes, surviving harsh EM noise on simulated Martian terrain. * **CubeSat payloads & research internships** across Switzerland and Dubai; presented an environmental sensing platform at the UN Geneva Forum.

Beyond oscilloscopes and firmware debuggers, I fly FPV quadcopters and mentor high‑school robotics teams.

Tech stack: C/C++, Rust, Python, FreeRTOS, Zephyr, CST, KiCad, GitHub Actions.

Languages: English & French (fluent), Spanish (intermediate).
