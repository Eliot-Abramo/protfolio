About Me

I’m an Electrical & Electronics Engineering graduate from EPFL with a passion for pushing hardware to its limits – whether that’s squeezing micro‑amp budgets from CubeSat payload controllers or designing CRC‑validated SPI stacks that shrug off Mars‑rover EM noise.

Recent roles include Embedded Systems Engineer at Spacelocker (CubeSat data‑handling), Lead System Engineer for the EPFL Xplore Rover, and research on analog neural networks at EPFL’s Laboratory of Wave Engineering.

Fluent in C/C++, Rust, Python, and KiCad; comfortable from schematic capture to HAL drivers and CI pipelines. Bilingual English–French, conversational Spanish.

Outside the lab you’ll find me racing quadcopters or teaching high‑school robotics teams.
